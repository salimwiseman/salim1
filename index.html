about me

This is Salim Al Mushaifri, MBA, CFE, CCP, CFC, CRBA, Int. Dip. (FinCrime). I am skilled and experienced Risk Management Expert with substantial experience of two decades in Internal Auditing (including IT Auditing), Financial Crimes Prevention, Fraud Investigations, and Operational Risk Assessments. In addition, my experience extends to Computer Programming, IT Project Management, and System Design and Development.


Over the years, I worked in a number of businesses and banks in Oman, and worked in all control functions, including Internal Audit Department, Compliance Department, and Risk Management Department. Currently I hold the position of Head of Anti-Fraud Department in Bank Dhofar. I also held the position of the Chairman of the Audit Committee of Oman Riders Club. Furthermore, I am currently a Board Member in the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners, ACFE, Oman Chapter, and I have participated in organizing Anti-Fraud conferences.

I hold 7 academic and professional qualifications including Master in Business Administration (MBA), International Diploma in Financial Crime Prevention (Int. Dip.FinCrime), Certified Risk-based Auditor (CRBA), Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE), Certified in Financial Crime (CFC), and Certified Compliance Professional (CCP).

From Social Responsibility perspective, I have delivered many Anti-Fraud and Operational Risk Management Trainings, Workshops, Lectures, and webinars to the Public, as well as to Private, Governmental, and Professional Organizations, including State Audit of Oman, Public Authority for Social Insurance (PASI), Crowe Oman,




Oman Development Bank, Bank Dhofar, National Bank of Oman, the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners (ACFE), the Information Systems Audit and Control Association (ISACA), and the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI).

Furthermore, I have been participating as a panelist in multiple Panel Discussions and I have been delivering multiple professional trainings and workshops to the Banking Industry in Oman in coordination with the College of Banking Financial Studies.
